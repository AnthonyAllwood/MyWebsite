BIOGRAPHY

Who is Anthony Allwood?

Anthonyis a hard-working and dedicated student, leader, and athlete who strives for success and puts in the utmost effort in order to achieve his goals. He is an aspring computer engineer, and a senior attending the State University of New York at New Paltz in pursuit of a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering. Anthony has spent the majority of his life as a student athlete who excels in his educational journey with a passion for technological devices and processes while maintaining his love and ability to play basketball and workout in the gym. He portrays extreme appreciation and interest in how technology works and the complex processes that allow devices to function and benefit various users and companies. He has also shown an interest and desire to help come up with solutions to problems and errors that users may have with devices such as computers and cell phones.

Anthony has taken part in various activities and projects that have helped him gain the knowledge and experience that is needed to succeed in his career. He is currently an active member of the National Society of Black Engineers (NSBE) which is a non-profit educational organization that supports and promotes the aspirations of collegiate and pre-collegiate students and technical professionals in engineering and technology. Anthony is also the captain of his Senior Design Project team which involves the creation of a security drone that can fly mid to high altitudes and record information with a modified camera. Anthony also had the opportunity to work as a Computer Engineering/Information Technology intern at the non-profit educational organization in which he created and developed numerous websites and mobile applications, as well as working with potential clients and people of influence.
